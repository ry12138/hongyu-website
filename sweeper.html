Product Features

Five-in-One Cleaning Solution

Integrating sweeping, washing, vacuuming, dust suppression, and high-pressure cleaning into a single compact machine.

Vacuum Sweeping, Ultra-Efficient

Pure vacuum suction system with powerful four-brush hydraulic drive. Self-adjusting brush pressure thoroughly removes debris and dust of all sizes while extending brush life.

Integrated High-Pressure Washing

Front and side high-pressure flush systems with 100-120 bar water gun. Simultaneous sweep-and-wash capability handles stubborn stains and small ads instantly.

Rear-Wheel Steering, 360° Turn

Front-wheel drive with rear-wheel steering enables curb climbing, on-the-spot U-turns, and effortless navigation through the narrowest urban alleys with a 1.95m turning radius.

Ultra-Low Cost, Replace 10+ Workers

Total operating cost including electricity and maintenance is less than 1/5 of fuel vehicles. One machine replaces over 10 manual workers, saving significant labor costs annually.

Portable Vacuum Attachment

Optional extended vacuum hose for cleaning dead corners like flower beds, sidewalk drains, and other areas the main unit cannot reach — achieving true one-stop cleaning.

Smart Comfort Cabin

Integrated HVAC air conditioning, smart dashboard, reverse camera, cruise control, hill-start assist, and car-grade audio — providing a comfortable working environment year-round.
