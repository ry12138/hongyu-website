About Hongyu

Water is Life,
We Nurture Cities

Founded in 2003, Hongyu Heavy Industry is a professional manufacturer integrating R&D, production, sales, and after-sales service for water sprinkler trucks. Our facility covers over 50,000 m² with modern production lines and strict quality control systems.

Adhering to the philosophy of "Quality First, Innovation Driven", our product lineup spans 5-ton to 25-ton sprinkler trucks, widely used in municipal sanitation, landscaping, mining dust suppression, and construction.
